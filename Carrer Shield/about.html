About Career Shield

Our Mission

We are dedicated to creating a safer job market for everyone. With the rise of remote work, job scams have become increasingly sophisticated. Career Shield was founded to provide job seekers with the tools they need to verify opportunities and avoid fraud.

Our mission is to empower every job seeker with the knowledge and technology to distinguish between genuine opportunities and malicious traps. We believe that safety in the job market is a fundamental right.

Our team of cybersecurity experts and HR professionals work around the clock to maintain our database of verified companies and known scams.

Why We Started

After seeing countless talented individuals fall victim to fake job offers, we decided enough was enough. We built Career Shield to be the first line of defense for your career.

In 2024 alone, job scams resulted in over $300 million in losses for job seekers. We aim to bring that number down to zero.
